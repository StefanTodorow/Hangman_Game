Rules of Hangman game

The rules are simple

  1. Think of one letter that can fit in the word.

  2. Insert your letter in the textbox below the hidden word.

  3. Click ENTER or click on the button to try the letter.

  4. Repeat the process until you win or lose.

  5. Enjoy!

If you have any issues with the game or want to report a bug, please contact me in the Contacts page. Thank you!
